Recipe for crêpes

Description

Simple recipe of delicious crêpes.

Ingredients

  • 700 ml milk
  • 400 g flour
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 80 g butter
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 g salt

Steps

  1. Add the eggs to a bowl and beat them.
  2. Add the brown sugar, salt, and mix.
  3. Melt the butter.
  4. Add the flour and melted butter to the mixture, stirring everything until you get a thick dough.
  5. Add one-third of the milk and mix well. Repeat the process.
  6. Let it rest for 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  7. Final step place a pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with a bit of butter.
  8. Cook until the edges start to lift from the pan and the surface is set, about 1-2 minutes.
  9. Flip the crêpe and cook the other side for an additional 30 seconds to 1 minute.
Done!

This classic recipe of french gastronomy is ready to be enjoyed !
